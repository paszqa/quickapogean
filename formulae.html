Formulae

Next level XP

Total XP that makes you advance to the next level.

XP = 50 x CurrentLevel x Current Level x (1 + (CurrentLevel/2))

Staff formula

Weapon Damage + Magic/5

Spell formula

((SpellBaseDamage + Magic) x (1 + SpellMultiplier/100))

Auto attack raw damage formula

((WeaponDamage x (1 + Ability/100)) - TargetDefense)/(1 + TargetArmor/100)

Ability formula

Increases the damage of weapon damage, weapon spells, and increases your minimum damage.

WeaponDamage x (1 + Ability/100)

Attack Interval formula

Calculate the time between auto attacks.

2 seconds / (AttackSpeed / 10)

Bow formula

WeaponDamage + MoveSpeed/5 + AttackSpeed/5

Regeneration

Regeneration ticks every 10 seconds.

Regenerated amount is equal to HP Regen and MP Regen stats.

Attack range in cubes formula

Every 10 points increase your Range by 1 cube.

Range/10

Armor formula

Every 2 Armor points reduce physical damage taken by 1 point flat plus about 2%, but diminishing returns apply.

(Attacker Damage - Armor/2) / (1 + Armor/100)

Defense formula

Every 1 point in Defense points reduces physical damage taken by 1 point flat in 1 vs 1 encounter.

(Attacker Damage - Defense) / (NumberOfAttackers)
